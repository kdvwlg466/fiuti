## The SKAG Advantage (and Why It Matters)

If you're not familiar with SKAGs, here's the quick version: Single Keyword Ad Groups mean having one keyword per ad group instead of lumping multiple keywords together. This lets you create hyper-targeted ads that match exactly what people search for.

Why bother? Higher Quality Scores, better click-through rates, and lower CPCs. When your ad headline exactly matches what someone types into Google, they're more likely to click.

The catch is that SKAGs are tedious to set up manually. You need separate ad groups for each keyword, different match types for each, and negative keywords to prevent ad groups from competing against each other. Miss one negative keyword and your ad groups start cannibalizing each other's impressions.

Fiuti handles this automatically. You input your keywords, and it generates the entire structure with proper negative keyword lists in minutes.
